Designing real-time intelligence for high-pressure decisions

An AI platform that supports agents exactly when decisions matter.

The problem

Decisions were made under pressure, without support

Customer support agents handled complex conversations in real time, switching between tools while trying to respond quickly and accurately.

This created cognitive overload, slower responses and inconsistent outcomes.

Insight

Intelligence is only valuable
when it arrives at the right moment

From tools → to real-time guidance

Instead of giving agents more interfaces, the system was redesigned to deliver contextual assistance within the flow of the conversation.

Contextual AI suggestions

Real-time prompts based on conversation context, reducing the need to search for answers.

Unified workspace

All tools and data brought into a single interface, eliminating constant switching.

Automated summaries

Calls are documented automatically, freeing agents to focus on the conversation.

Impact

+35%Efficiency
-40%Handling time
+22%Resolution rate

Reflection

AI should not replace decisions.
It should support them.

The value of AI is not in automation alone, but in helping humans act with clarity under pressure.
