Designing control in complex connected environments

A smart home experience that simplifies how users interact with devices.

The problem

Users were managing devices, not outcomes

Smart home ecosystems introduced more devices, but also more complexity and fragmentation.

Users had to think about individual devices instead of what they wanted to achieve.

Insight

People don’t think in devices.
They think in outcomes.

From device control → to system orchestration

The experience was redesigned to focus on actions and scenarios, rather than individual device interactions.

Unified control hub

All devices accessible from a single, structured interface.

Automation scenarios

Users create routines based on goals, not device states.

Clear system feedback

Every action provides immediate and understandable feedback.

Impact

-30%Task time
+22%Engagement
+18%Automation usage

Reflection

Control is not about options.
It is about clarity.

In complex ecosystems, simplicity comes from structuring interactions, not removing functionality.
